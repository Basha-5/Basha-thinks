HOW IT WORKS

1 — Face Model Initialization

TinyFaceDetector and FaceLandmark68Net models load from CDN. These lightweight neural networks (~2MB total) run entirely in WebGL via TensorFlow.js.

2 — Enrollment (5 Samples)

During registration, 5 face descriptor samples are captured and averaged. Averaging reduces noise and environmental variation for a more robust identity template.

3 — Feature Extraction

FaceRecognitionNet processes each frame through 28 convolutional layers to output a 128-float descriptor vector representing the unique geometry of your face.

4 — Identity Matching

Login computes the Euclidean distance between the live face descriptor and stored enrollment descriptors. Distance < 0.55 confirms identity; > 0.55 rejects.

5 — Credential + Biometric Fusion

Both the password hash and biometric match must pass simultaneously. Either factor alone is insufficient — this is genuine two-factor authentication fusion.
